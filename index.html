About Me

My name is Umut Kocak, and I am a seasoned professional in computer graphics and computer vision, with a career that began in 2004. My journey, which started with image processing, has led me through a diverse array of projects, including registration, segmentation, volume rendering, haptic rendering, physics engines, real-time physically-based rendering, photogrammetry, 3D reconstruction, SLAM, ray tracing, modern computer vision, and machine learning.

I find joy in understanding the intricate relationships between mathematical models and real-world phenomena, especially those involving visual imagination. I have mostly worked on demanding real-time interactive projects, and as a result, I have developed expertise in GPU programming, code optimization, and memory monitoring. I enjoy seeing not only the big picture but also paying attention to details, as the saying goes: "The devil is in the details."

Having worked extensively with classical graphical rendering and computer vision algorithms, I have also developed a strong interest in modern techniques like deep learning. In challenging cases, I believe that combining classical and modern approaches can complement each other, at least at the time of writing, to achieve the best results. I am particularly interested in Diffusion Models and Neural Radiance Fields (NERFs).

Apart from technical challenges, I have occasionally taken on diverse roles, including teaching, mentorship, project management, Scrum Master, and team lead. I enjoy socializing in person with colleagues and sharing silly jokes.

I prefer to spend my free time actively outdoors. In the past, I’ve enjoyed snowboarding, playing tennis, and running, but lately, my knees have been telling me to find other hobbies. Therefore, I have been focusing on more sustainable activities like freestyle swimming and sailing. My lifelong passion also includes partner dances such as salsa, bachata, and zouk.

After living in different countries, I eventually settled in Berlin, where I started my own family and discovered my latest passion—spending time outdoors with my son.💗
